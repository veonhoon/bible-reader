Overview

Bible Reader is a free app that delivers daily biblical teachings and scripture. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or registration.

We may collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics, including:

  • General app usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for daily teaching reminders. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal data. Anonymous analytics data is processed by Google Firebase.

Data Storage

Reading progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.
